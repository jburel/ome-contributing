FS Screen/Plate/Well
======================



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check a plate/screen (with multiple Fields per well) import and viewing

.. only:: html

      **Download both Gretzky and Howe builds and compare the speed of the workflow on the same images on Gretzky vs Howe. Test ALL plates pre-loaded on Gretzky and Howe, see:**
      `FS testing table <https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0AoMHD4RIe-94dHJabm9UWVo0VzRqVnhBalk1eEJKZlE#gid=4>`_ 

.. only:: latex

      **Compare the speed of the workflow on the same images on FS-demo build vs non-FS build (e.g. 4.4.6).** 



#. Double-click on Plate or Plate Run.

   - Both actions should display in the central panel.[:ref:`FSspwscreen002`][:ref:`FSspwscreen003`]

#. Double-click on a well/field to open viewer [:ref:`FSspwscreen004`][:ref:`FSspwscreen005`]

#. Select a new Field in the dropdown menu above the central panel [:ref:`FSspwscreen006`]

   - All thumbnails should be updated.

#. Toggle on and off the icon allowing you to magnify a thumbnail while mousing over it.[:ref:`FSspwscreen007`] (Insight only)

#. Open the view of all Fields - icon to the left of field choice menu, above central panel.[:ref:`FSspwscreen008`] (Insight only)

   - Select "Spatial" view [:ref:`FSspwscreen009`][:ref:`FSspwscreen010`]
   - |C| that the spatial relation of the fields is displayed properly.
   - Select "As a row" view [:ref:`FSspwscreen011`]
