Share
======



**To test in**: Web

**Purpose of the test** is to check the Share feature in Web, creation, visibility, and annotation possibility

#. Select images add to basket [:ref:`ShareScreen001`].

#. Create a share with several users: In the central window, click on the basket icon, choose "Manage basket"[:ref:`ShareScreen002`], choose images to share and use the "Globe" icon to create a share [:ref:`ShareScreen003`]. Choose an expiry date one day after creation [:ref:`ShareScreen004`][:ref:`ShareScreen005`]

#. Go Public tab (left-hand Panel). Select the share. Try to edit (pencil symbol - General tab, right pane).[:ref:`ShareScreen006`][:ref:`ShareScreen007`]

   - |C| that the number of images in the share [right-hand panel] is correct i.e. Share Count [:ref:`ShareScreen008`]
   - |C| than images in the share can be viewed using the Preview and Full viewer [:ref:`ShareScreen009`]
   - |C| that images in the share cannot be annotated (i.e. rate, tags etc.) = the General tab has no choices in its lower part.[:ref:`ShareScreen010`]
