Share
======

.. |C| replace:: **Check**

To test in: 

Web


Purpose of the test is to check the Share feature in Web, creation, visibility, and annotation possibility

1. Select images add to basket [:download:`001 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/001.png>`].

2. Create a share with several users: In the central window, click on the basket icon, choose "Manage basket"[:download:`002 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/002.png>`], choose images to share and use the "Globe" icon to create a share [:download:`003 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/003.png>`]. Choose an expiry date one day after creation [:download:`004 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/004.png>`, :download:`005 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/005.png>`].

3. Go Public tab (left-hand Panel). Select the share. Try to edit (pencil symbol - General tab, right pane).[:download:`006 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/006.png>`, :download:`007 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/007.png>`]

 - |C| Check that the number of images in the share [right-hand panel] is correct i.e. Share Count [:download:`008 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/008.png>`]
 - |C| Check than images in the share can be viewed using the Preview and Full viewer [:download:`009 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/009.png>`]
 - |C| Check that images in the share cannot be annotated (i.e. rate, tags etc.) = the General tab has no choices in its lower part.[:download:`010 </images/testing_scenarios/Share/010.png>`]
