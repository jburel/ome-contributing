Browse/Filter/Preview
======================



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check the browsing/filtering/preview feature in clients and to check the sensibility of appropriate command menus

#. Browse dataset/ Plate.

#. Try to filter by rating/tags, date. [:ref:`BrowseFPScreen001`][:download:`001 </images/testing_scenarios/BrowseFilterPreview/001.png>`]

#. Switch between list and thumbnail view.[:ref:`BrowseFPScreen002`][:download:`002 </images/testing_scenarios/BrowseFilterPreview/002.png>`]

#. If applicable

   - Sort the data by name
   - |C| that right-click menu options are the same in Browser and DataManager (Insight only). [:ref:`BrowseFPScreen003`, :ref:`BrowseFPScreen004`][:download:`003 </images/testing_scenarios/BrowseFilterPreview/003.png>`, :download:`004 </images/testing_scenarios/BrowseFilterPreview/004.png>`]
   - Create new dataset from selection
   - Double-click on image to open viewer.

#. |C| multi-selection i.e. shift - key down.

#. |C| that DataManager and right-hand panel are correctly synchronized and updated.

#. In Preview (right-hand of the window, "Preview" tab) [:ref:`BrowseFPScreen005`][:download:`005 </images/testing_scenarios/BrowseFilterPreview/005.png>`]: if applicable

   - Modify the settings of the selected image.
   - Apply the settings to the selection.
