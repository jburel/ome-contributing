View Big Image
===============



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check viewing/rendering of large images (tiles, bird's eye features)

#. Open a large image

   - |C| that location of bird's eye corresponds with the image part shown while loading of tiles.[:ref:`ViewBig001`][:ref:`ViewBig002`]

#. Modify the resolution level using slider on the left (Insight) [:ref:`ViewBig004`] or mousewheel (Web) [:ref:`ViewBig005`]

   - |C| tile loading

#. Modify rendering settings [:ref:`ViewBig006`]
