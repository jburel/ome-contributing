View Big Image
===============



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check viewing/rendering of large images (tiles, bird's eye features)

#. Open a large image

   - |C| that location of bird's eye corresponds with the image part shown while loading of tiles.[:ref:`ViewBigScreen001`][:ref:`ViewBigScreen002`][:ref:`ViewBigScreen003`][:download:`001 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBigImage/001.png>`, :download:`002 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBigImage/002.png>`, :download:`003 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBigImage/003.png>`]

#. Modify the resolution level using slider on the left (Insight) [:ref:`ViewBigScreen004`][:download:`004 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBigImage/004.png>`] or mousewheel (Web) [:ref:`ViewBigScreen005`][:download:`005 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBigImage/005.png>`]

   - |C| tile loading

#. Modify rendering settings [:ref:`ViewBigScreen006`][:download:`006 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBigImage/006.png>`]
