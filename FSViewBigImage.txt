FS View Big Image
==================



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check viewing/rendering of large images (tiles, bird's eye features) for FS demo. Concentrate on performance.
**Download both Gretzky and Howe builds and compare the speed of the workflow on the same images on Gretzky vs Howe. Test ALL Big images pre-loaded on Gretzky and Howe, see:**
`FS testing table <https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0AoMHD4RIe-94dHJabm9UWVo0VzRqVnhBalk1eEJKZlE#gid=4>`_ 

#. Open a large image

   - |C| that location of bird's eye corresponds with the image part shown while loading of tiles.[:ref:`FSViewBigScreen001`][:ref:`FSViewBigScreen002`][:ref:`FSViewBigScreen003`]

#. Modify the resolution level using slider on the left (Insight) [:ref:`FSViewBigScreen004`] or mousewheel (Web) [:ref:`FSViewBigScreen005`]

   - |C| tile loading

#. Modify rendering settings [:ref:`FSViewBigScreen006`]
