User/Group Manipulation
========================



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check the viewing/display of groups/users and their data

#. Add several groups to the display (Insight only) Switch between groups. [:ref:`UserGroupMScreen001`][:ref:`UserGroupMScreen002`][:ref:`UserGroupMScreen003`]

#. Add several users to the display [:ref:`UserGroupMScreen002`][:ref:`UserGroupMScreen005`] (Insight only)

#. Switch between users.

#. Browse the user's data - go to the tree in left-hand pane, expand projects and datasets, view images. Go to the Screens harmonica and browse screens.

#. Remove/Refresh users from/on display.(Insight only) - use both menu (remove) [:ref:`UserGroupMScreen002`] and right-click (remomve & refresh) [:ref:`UserGroupMScreen004`]

#. Remove groups from display.(Insight only)

#. Log off and log in as a new user. Bear in mind the danger of clashes with other testers when picking a new username.

   - |C| that you can browse data
   - |C| that you can view images.

#. |C| that you can see annotations on other user's objects (P/D/I/S/Plate/Plate Run) as appropriate.
