ROI Web
========

.. |C| replace:: **Check**

To test in:

Web


Purpose of the test is to check the ROI viewing in Web

1. Open in Viewer an image with >3 ROIs propagated through multiple z/t (If not exist, create it in Insight.)

2. View the ROIs [:download:`001 </images/testing_scenarios/ROIWeb/001.png>`, :download:`002 </images/testing_scenarios/ROIWeb/002.png>`]

3. Expand and collapse the multiple-shape ROIs

4. Choose ROIs on different z/t planes

 - |C| Check that the Viewer updates

5. Tick and untick the Text and Preview boxes [:download:`003 </images/testing_scenarios/ROIWeb/003.png>`]

 - |C| Check the text in the ROIs appear/disappears and the Preview is made visible/invisible in all ROIs and shapes on different planes.

6. Hover with mouse over the Thumbnail in Preview

 - |C| Check that larger view appears.[:download:`004 </images/testing_scenarios/ROIWeb/004.png>`]
