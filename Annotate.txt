Annotate
========



**To test in**: Web, Insight

**Purpose of the test** is to check the Annotate ability (creating as well as viewing) for P/D/I/Plates/Plate Acquisitions

#. Annotate different type of objects i.e. Projects/Datasets/Images/Plates/Plate Acquisitions (=Plate Run)

   - Comments (right-hand pane [:ref:`AnnotateScreen001`][:download:`001 </images/testing_scenarios/Annotate/001.png>`])

   - Rating (right-hand pane)

   - Attachments (right-hand pane)

   - Tags (both left-hand pane right-click and right hand pane possible)

#. Select an annotated object

#. Go to the right-hand side and mouse over the annotation.

   - |C| that the owner of the annotation is indicated, who annotated etc.[:ref:`AnnotateScreen002`, :ref:`AnnotateScreen003`][:download:`002 </images/testing_scenarios/Annotate/002.png>`, :download:`003 </images/testing_scenarios/Annotate/003.png>`]
