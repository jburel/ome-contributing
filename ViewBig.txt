View Big
=========

.. |C| replace:: **Check**

Purpose of the test is to check viewing/rendering of large images (tiles, bird's eye features)



1. Open a large image

 - |C| Check that location of bird's eye corresponds with the image part shown while loading of tiles.[:download:`001 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBig/001.png>`, :download:`002 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBig/002.png>`, :download:`003 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBig/003.png>`]

2. Modify the resolution level using slider on the left (Insight) [:download:`004 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBig/004.png>`] or mousewheel (Web) [:download:`005 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBig/005.png>`]

 - |C| Check tile loading

3. Modify rendering settings [:download:`006 </images/testing_scenarios/ViewBig/006.png>`]
